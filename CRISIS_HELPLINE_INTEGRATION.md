# 🆘 Crisis Helpline Integration - Complete

## ✅ What Was Added

The AI assistant now:

1. **Detects Serious Concerns**
   - Suicidal thoughts or self-harm
   - Immediate danger
   - Severe crisis situations

2. **Provides Helpline Numbers Immediately**
   - **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline**: Call or text 988 (24/7)
   - **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741 (24/7)
   - **National Suicide Prevention Lifeline**: 1-800-273-8255 (24/7)

3. **Responds with Deep Empathy**
   - Validates feelings
   - Shows genuine care
   - Provides immediate support
   - Still offers scheduling help

---

## 🎯 How It Works

### When Patient Mentions Serious Concerns

**Patient says:** "I've been having thoughts of hurting myself"

**AI responds:**
```
I'm so sorry you're going through this. Your feelings are valid, and I want you to know that help is available right now.

**If you're in immediate danger, please reach out to one of these helplines:**

• **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline**: Call or text **988** (available 24/7)
• **Crisis Text Line**: Text **HOME to 741741** (available 24/7)
• **National Suicide Prevention Lifeline**: **1-800-273-8255** (available 24/7)

You don't have to go through this alone. These helplines have trained counselors who can help you right now.

I'm also here to help you find a therapist for ongoing support when you're ready. Would you like me to help you schedule an appointment?
```

---

## 📋 Required Information Collection

The AI will now **gently ask for required information**:

### Insurance
"I'd like to help match you with a therapist who accepts your insurance. **Which insurance provider do you have?**"

### Scheduling
"**What days and times work best for you?** I want to make sure we find a time that fits your schedule."

### Contact Information
"To schedule your appointment, I'll need:
• **Your full name**
• **Email address**
• **Phone number**

Is it okay if I collect this information?"

The AI now:

- **Validates feelings**: "I can hear that this is really difficult for you"
